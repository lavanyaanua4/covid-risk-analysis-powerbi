# Covid Patient Risk & Severity Analysis — Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Covid patient data to identify high-risk groups, severity factors, and healthcare burden using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal is to support data-driven decision-making for healthcare planning and risk assessment.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify most affected age groups
- Analyze hospitalization trends
- Evaluate impact of pre-existing medical conditions
- Detect high-risk patient profiles
- Provide actionable healthcare insights

---

## 📂 Dataset Information
The dataset includes patient demographics and medical conditions:

- Age
- Gender
- Hospitalization status
- Pneumonia
- Diabetes
- Asthma
- COPD
- Cardiovascular disease
- Obesity
- Renal disease
- ICU admission
- Mortality indicators

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop
- Data Modeling & DAX
- Data Visualization
- Healthcare Analytics

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🧓 Age Risk Analysis
Identifies which age groups are most affected and most vulnerable.

### 🏥 Hospitalization Trends
Analyzes whether hospitalization increases with age.

### ⚠️ Comorbidity Impact
Evaluates how pre-existing conditions influence severity.

### 🫁 Pneumonia & ICU Analysis
Examines complications associated with critical outcomes.

### 🧍 Gender Distribution
Compares cases and outcomes across genders.

### 📈 Key Performance Indicators
- Total Patients
- Total Deaths
- Mortality Rate
- ICU Patients
- Positive Cases

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Older patients show significantly higher hospitalization rates
- Patients with comorbidities are at greater risk of severe outcomes
- Pneumonia strongly correlates with critical cases
- Mortality increases with age and underlying conditions
- Certain groups require prioritized healthcare resources
